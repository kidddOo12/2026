Salary Negotiation Scripts
Your Numbers
  • Floor: ₹9L — never go below this
  • Target: ₹11–12L — what you aim for
  • Stretch: ₹14–15L — GCC bank roles
  • Current: ₹4.5L — never reveal first
Never Do This
  • Never accept on the spot — always 24hr
  • Never reveal current salary if avoidable
  • Never give a single number — give a range
  • Never apologise for negotiating
Non-Negotiable Rules
Daily Non-Negotiables
  • English investigation narration: 7 days a week, no exceptions. This is your #1 interview bottleneck.
  • SQL: minimum 5 sessions per week in Month 1. Reduces to 3–4 in Month 2.
  • Update job tracker every time you apply or get a response.
  • Never stop applying until offer letter is physically signed.
  • Record every mock interview. Watch it back. Growth is visible this way.
Plan Killers — Avoid These
  • Skipping English because you are tired — this is when the habit actually forms.
  • Applying before Resume Version A is locked — one weak application to a top company closes that door for months.
  • Accepting the first offer without negotiating even once.
  • Stopping applications once interviews start — the pipeline is your leverage.
  • Applying to KYC-only roles — you are already above that level.
AI-Proof Career Path (Long Term)
Year 1–2: Merchant Risk / FC Analyst at fintech-direct. Own investigation quality, start touching risk policies.

Year 2–4: Senior Analyst. Move toward risk governance — why rules exist, what typologies are missed, how to reduce false positives.

Year 4–6: Financial Crime Advisory (Big 4) or Risk Policy Lead (global fintech). International mobility opens here.

Year 6+: UAE → Singapore → UK / Ireland. MLRO track or Financial Crime Consulting Lead.
